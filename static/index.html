about me

I like to think I have a varied and well-rounded quiver of hobbies. I'm constantly reminding myself not to start anything new! I enjoy irregular schedules, rock climbing, hiking very slowly, begrudginly cycling, wishing running didn't hurt, reading library books and the periodic video game binge.

One of the hobbies, however, that I have enjoyed the longest is working with fibers. I have practiced both knitting and crocheting for many years. My collection of needles and hooks has been handed down for generations, and I think fondly of the women who came before me with every project that comes to life.
